Jeju Island


Jeju Island is a popular tourist destination in South Korea, known for its natural beauty, unique culture, and easy access via public transport. Here are some must-visit places that are conveniently accessible by public transportation:



Places in Jeju Island you can visit

- Famous Tourist Spots -


Seongsan Ilchulbong(Sunrise Peak)

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this tuff cone crater is famous for its stunning sunrise views.


Hallasan National Park

Home to South Korea's highest mountain, Hallasan, this park offers numerous hiking trails with breathtaking views.


Jeju Folk Village Museum

A cultural vilage that showcases traditional Korean life and architecture


Manjanggul Cave

One of the longest lava tubes in the world, featuring spectacular volcanic formations


Cheonjeyeon Waterfalls

A series of three waterfalls with beautiful surrounding scenery


Jusangjeolli Cliff

Stunning volcanic rock formations along the coas


Seogwipo Maeil Olle Market

A bustling traditional market offering local food, souvenirs, and more


Udo Island

It is a small island located off the eastern coast of Jeju Island, known for its beautiful landscapes, clear waters, and charming rural atmosphere. Visiting Udo is a popular day trip for tourists, and it is well-served by public transport.
